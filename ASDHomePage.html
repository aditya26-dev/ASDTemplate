Actuarial

Science Day

Actuarial Science Day (ASD) is an annual event organized by S1 Business Mathematics (Prasetiya Mulya University). This event aims to broaden the knowledge of high school students regarding actuarial science and the job opportunites of an actuary in the future. This year, the ASD implementation will be made online which includes 4 main activities, namely 3 in 1 competition (Escape room, Relay Exam, and Case Analysis), Boot Camp, Workshop, and Talk Show.

Vision

To change society's view on mathematics which is assured to be difficult, and to develop students' interests and their talents in the field of mathematics, especially in actuarial science.

Mission

  • To socialize about actuarial science which is currently developing and has good prospects in the future to the society.
  • To provide education and to open people's eyes that mathematics can be a solution in increasing the efficiency and effectiveness of all aspects including managing risks in a fun way.
  • Organizing activities that can help creativity and to develop the application of mathematics and actuarial science in problem solving.
