Actuarial

Science Day

Actuarial Science Day (ASD) is an annual event organized by S1 Business Mathematics (Prasetiya Mulya University). This event aims to broaden the knowledge of high school students regarding actuarial science and the job opportunites of an actuary in the future. This year, the ASD implementation will be made online which includes 4 main activities, namely 3 in 1 competition (Escape room, Relay Exam, and Case Analysis), Boot Camp, Workshop, and Talk Show.

Vision

To change society's view on mathematics which is assured to be difficult, and to develop students' interests and their talents in the field of mathematics, especially in actuarial science.

Mission

  • To socialize about actuarial science which is currently developing and has good prospects in the future to the society.
  • To provide education and to open people's eyes that mathematics can be a solution in increasing the efficiency and effectiveness of all aspects including managing risks in a fun way.
  • Organizing activities that can help creativity and to develop the application of mathematics and actuarial science in problem solving.

FAQs

Who can join Actuarial Science Day 2021? Can I participate in all events?

ASD is an open-entry national event that will be held online, thus everyone is welcome to participate in ASD's Workshop. Talk Shows, and Game (visit our battle page to help Matt save Metta!). However, there are terms and conditions for those who want to participate in ASD's competition

I am interested to be a candidate, but I don't really understand math, should I register myself?

Yes, you should! ASD's event is held for everyone. In this event, you will go through a step by step learning journey. Before combating in ASD's 3 in 1 competition, you will be trained in the Boot Camp. Moreover, we will hold a workshop and talk shows brought by experts, so don't forget to join all the events!

What is the Competition System?

- ASD’s 3 in 1 Competition consists of 2 elimination rounds + 1 grand final. The eliminations are in the form of escape room and relay exam, meanwhile the grand final is in the form of case analysis.
- ASD’s 3 in 1 competition is a team challenge, where each team consists of 3 candidates. Since the competition material combines social science and science lessons, a combination of 2 candidates from science major and 1 candidate from social science major is recommended.

What is the Scoring System?

Teams who will proceed to the next level are those with the highest points. Points will be reset for every level.
1. Escape Room: points will be calculated based on the time taken to complete tasks, the farthest room reached, and the speed of doing the tasks sequentially.
2. Relay Exam: after submission, points will be calculated in accumulation based on the numbers of correct and wrong answer of all candidates in the team
3. Case Analysis: points will be calculated based on the Headquarters’ scoring of the process and analysis result, presentation skills, and the ability to answer the given questions.

Can a school register more than 1 team?

Yes, every school can register more than 1 team. Check out the bundling package!

If I am a candidate with Social Science major, can I register?

For sure! We do not restrict any majors to register.

Can I form a team with combination of candidates from social science and science major?

Yes, you can. The operation master highly recommends you to be allied with candidates from other major. Here is the thing about business mathematics: yes, it is about Math, but it also contains Business Economics in it. Thus, it is better to be prepared.

Can my team consists of students from different schools?

Yes. There are 2 registration flows:
a. School registration -> for teams whose candidates are enrolled in the same senior high school.
b. Private registration -> for teams whose candidates are enrolled in different schools.

For the course of the online events and competitions, what device should I prepare?

We recommend you to use a laptop/PC/tab to get the best experience . Don’t forget to also prepare external camera and stable internet connection.

If my school is outside of Java, can my team still join the competition?

Of course. All high school students in Indonesia are allowed to compete in ASD.

How much is the registration fee and is there any special price?

The normal price is 150k/team but you can get a special price if your team register early or if 2-3 teams (from the same school) register together in ASD. Quick, head on to our website to register your team! Or check out our Instagram @asd.prasmul for more info regarding the entry fee.

Can candidates from different grades be in one team?

Yes, as long as all of the candidates’ status are high school students in the academic year 2021/2022.

Will the competition be in English?

For level 1, the Operation Master will use English, but some questions will be in Bahasa Indonesia. The rest will be in Bahasa Indonesia. However, for level 3, you are free to use English if you prefer to do so. However, please highlight that you should be consistent with the usage of formal language. You are free to browse for translations in the internet.

Can a team consist of 2 candidates?

No, a team must consist of 3 candidates because the competition is set for 3 candidates per team.

- Learn to analyze a team's capacity and how maximize its ability in order to work effectively and efficiently
- Gain experience in processing real company's data
