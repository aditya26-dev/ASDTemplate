S1 Business Mathematics

Universitas Prasetiya Mulya

Where Analytics Build Enterprises


Undergraduate Business Mathematics program aims to develop students’ competencies and create graduates with broad insights in business and proficiency in analytics and computation. The program consists of approximately two-third applied mathematics and statistics courses and one-third business courses.

Decision-making with principles of mathematical modelling and statistics

Data-driven modelling with sophisticated computer modelling system

Development of business world becomes increasingly complex. Business decisions driven by data through effective and efficient modelling, analysis, and computation becomes an absolute necessity. Expertise in mathematics is now essential in business and professional world. Thus, mathematics should not be avoided because it seems intimidating. On the contrary, mathematics is enjoyable and highly-demanded in various business industries.

    Actuarial Mathematics

    Actuarial Mathematics is a discipline which applies theories of mathematics, statistics, computation, and economics to evaluate financial risks, specifically in pension funds and insurance industry, as well as other financial sectors. This competency will groom the students to become a professional Actuary.

    Financial Mathematics

    Financial Mathematics is also known as Quantitative Finance or Financial Engineering. This discipline is closely related to the financial market. Various financial products and their problems are designed, developed and evaluated using mathematical, statistical and computational methods.

    Industrial Mathematics

    Industrial Mathematics is the application of mathematics which is closely related to engineering, science and industry. This discipline is developed to solve industrial issues, such as optimation, decision-making and management.


    Work Integrated Learning Cooperative Education (Co-Op)

    Graduates in Indonesia face great challenges because their higher education can no longer guarantee them a position of employment. Nowadays, companies demand graduates with working experience. Thus, graduates are not only expected to achieve excellence in academics, but it is important that they also possess the professional skills required to compete with other graduates from all around the world.

    Co-Op of Undergraduate Business Mathematics program is adopted from Co-Op of University of Waterloo Canada, which is currently the biggest Co-Op program in the world.

    01

    Co-Op program enables students to obtain at least one year of working experience in their field of expertise.

    02

    Co-Op students will study and work alternatingly in their respective work terms and study terms for a balanced experience.

    03

    While Co-Op students are working, they are considered as professionals who are given proper responsibilities and rights, much like full-time workers.

    Collaboration

    Universitas Prasetiya Mulya has signed a memorandum of understanding and have partnered with READI Project on 11th April 2017.

    READI (Risk Management, Economics Sustainability, and Actuarial Science Development in Indonesia) Project is a project implemented by University of Waterloo in Canada with the support from the Canadian government through Global Affairs Canada and Otoritas Jasa Keuangan (OJK).

    This project aims to increase the number and quality of actuarial graduates in Indonesia. READI Project is intended to strengthen human resources and sustainable economic development by providing the opportunity and support for fair recruitment, education and involvement in the actuary profession.
